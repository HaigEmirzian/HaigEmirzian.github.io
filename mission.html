Mission Statement

My mission is to accelerate society into the next wave of revolutionary technology. Born shortly after the Dot-Com bubble, I have grown parallel to the exponential growth of technology in this millennium. Technology has expedited and simplified life for billions, and continues to do so every year. It is inspiring and the reason for many to wake up every morning wondering what the next technological breakthrough will be. My goal is to clarify that next breakthrough.
